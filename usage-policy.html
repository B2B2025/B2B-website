Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Bharat2Business Platform-Wide AUP

This Acceptable Use Policy governs your use of all Bharat2Business services, including the Digital Partner, Government Scheme Discovery, Expert Network, and related features.

1. Purpose of the Platform

Bharat2Business provides:

  • A Digital Partner (AI-powered guidance system)
  • Government Scheme Discovery tools
  • Compliance checklists and documentation workflows
  • Verified Expert Network access
  • Trade show and growth opportunity discovery

The Platform is designed to support Indian MSMEs in lawful business activities only.

2. Misuse of the Digital Partner

The Digital Partner provides informational guidance tailored to MSME workflows. It is not a licensed professional advisor.

You may not:

  • Use the Digital Partner to seek assistance for illegal activities
  • Attempt to manipulate, reverse-engineer, or exploit system outputs
  • Prompt the Digital Partner to generate fraudulent documentation
  • Upload forged or misleading government or financial information
  • Use automated tools or bots to scrape or overload the system

Outputs from the Digital Partner are for informational purposes and do not constitute legal, financial, or regulatory advice.

3. Misuse of Government Scheme Discovery

The Government Scheme Discovery feature analyzes user-provided business data to identify potential eligibility.

You may not:

  • Submit false turnover, Udyam, GST, PAN, or registration information
  • Misrepresent your business profile to obtain eligibility results
  • Use scheme insights to engage in subsidy fraud
  • Upload manipulated documentation for government benefit applications

Bharat2Business does not guarantee approval under any scheme. Final eligibility is determined by relevant government authorities.

4. Misuse of Verified Expert Network

When interacting with professionals through Bharat2Business:

You may not:

  • Harass, threaten, or intimidate experts
  • Provide false or misleading business information
  • Solicit experts for unrelated commercial promotion
  • Circumvent platform safeguards to conduct unauthorized off-platform transactions

Experts must comply with professional standards and platform rules.

5. Platform Integrity

You may not:

  • Attempt unauthorized access to user accounts or system infrastructure
  • Introduce malicious software or code
  • Interfere with or degrade system performance
  • Use scraping, automated querying, or abusive API calls

6. Government Affiliation Disclaimer

Bharat2Business is an independent digital platform.

We are not a government body, nor are we officially affiliated with any ministry, department, or agency.

Government Scheme Discovery provides informational guidance only. Final decisions are made by the respective authorities.
