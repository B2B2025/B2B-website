Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bharat2Business ("we," "our," or "us") collects, processes, stores, uses, and discloses personal data when you access or use our website, mobile applications, Digital Partner (AI assistant), Government Scheme Discovery tools, Verified Expert Network, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using Bharat2Business, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our services to Indian MSMEs.

1.1 Information You Provide

At the time of registration or use of our Platform, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Company / business name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Postal address
  • Business details
  • Udyam Registration Number
  • GSTIN / PAN / CIN (if applicable)
  • Industry / sector information
  • Turnover details (if voluntarily submitted)

If you use paid services, we may collect:

  • Payment details
  • Billing information
  • Bank account details (processed securely via payment partners)

1.2 Information Generated Through Use

When you use the Platform, including the Digital Partner and Government Scheme Discovery tools, we may collect:

  • Queries submitted
  • Compliance workflows accessed
  • Expert interaction records
  • Chat transcripts
  • Usage patterns
  • Pages viewed
  • Session duration
  • Device type
  • IP address
  • Browser and operating system information

We may record communications with experts or support for quality assurance and service improvement purposes.

1.3 Sensitive Personal Data

We may process business-related statutory identifiers (GST, Udyam, PAN, etc.) where voluntarily provided for compliance or scheme discovery purposes.

We do not knowingly collect unrelated sensitive personal data unless required for lawful business verification.

1.4 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We and authorized partners may use:

  • Cookies
  • Pixel tags
  • Web beacons
  • Device identifiers

To:

  • Improve user experience
  • Enable personalization
  • Analyze platform performance
  • Provide relevant communications

You may control cookies through your browser settings.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use information for the following purposes:

  • To verify user identity
  • To provide compliance and business guidance
  • To power the Digital Partner
  • To analyze eligibility under Government Scheme Discovery
  • To match users with verified experts
  • To improve platform functionality
  • To prevent fraud and misuse
  • To communicate updates and notifications
  • To comply with legal obligations

If you object to certain processing activities, we may be unable to continue providing services.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data.

We may share information with:

3.1 Service Providers

Including:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Customer support systems

These parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.

3.2 Verified Experts

Where necessary for service delivery, relevant business information may be shared with experts engaged through the Platform.

3.3 Regulatory & Government Authorities

Where required under applicable laws, including:

  • Identity verification
  • Fraud prevention
  • Investigation of legal violations

3.4 Business Partners

Where explicitly consented by the user, we may share limited information with business partners for service enhancement.

4. Digital Partner & AI Data Use

Queries submitted to the Digital Partner may be processed to:

  • Generate contextual guidance
  • Improve system performance
  • Enhance response accuracy

We do not use user-specific business data to train public AI models.

AI-generated responses are informational and do not constitute professional advice.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data:

  • For as long as required to provide services
  • For compliance with statutory requirements
  • For fraud prevention and dispute resolution

Users may request deletion subject to applicable legal requirements.

Certain archival records may be retained for regulatory compliance.
